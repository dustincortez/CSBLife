Reflection Paper 5

Guide Questions and Reflection for NSTP Orientation:

1. What is the aim of NSTP?


	NSTP or the National Service Training Program aims to promote and integrate values education, tranformable 
leadership, spirit of patriotism and nationalism and the dignity of one's person needs.


2. What are the three (3) program components of NSTP? Differentiate the three.


	a.ROTC(Reserve Officers Training Corps)
		-a training program for the students to be prepared for military concerns.
	b.CWTS(Civic Welfare Training Service)
		-a training program for them to join or to participate to environmental issues/concerns.
	c.LTS(Literacy Training Service)
		-a training proframg for the students who would like to take part on the education of other kids.


3. What program component do you intend to enrol? Why?


	I want to enroll the LTS or also known as the Literacy Training Service because all I want is to help everybody or
even the children who can't study because of financial problems. I want to volunteer as their student training teacher for
them to learn what is intended for them.


4. What is the role of NSTP in nation-building?

	It can help the students open their eyes to the environment problems, or even the political and economic problems
that the country is facing right now. They can defend their own country to their enemies.


5. How will NSTP help you become good Filipino citizen and good Benildean-Lasallian?

	It can help me in doing what is right to verybody, I can also learn how to care to everyone and even our
environment.
