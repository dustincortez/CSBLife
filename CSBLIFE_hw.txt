10 things about St. Jean Baptist De La salle

1.He received the "tonsure" at age eleven. 
2.He was ordained to the priesthood at the age of 26 on April 9, 1678.
3.He became involved in education "little by little".
4.He was born on April 30,1651.
5.He was canonized on May 29, 1900 by Pope Leo XIII
6.He was the patron of all teachers of youth.
7.He was a priest, educational reformer.
8.He was the founder of Institute of the Brothers of the Christian Schools
9.He dedicated much of his life for the education of poor children in France.
10.He is considered the founder of the first Catholic schools.
