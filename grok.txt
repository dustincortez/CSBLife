What does "grok" or "grokking" mean?


	Grok means to understand so thoroughly that the observer becomes a part of the observed—to merge, blend,
intermarry, lose identity in group experience. It means almost everything that we mean by religion, philosophy, and
science—and it means as little to us as color means to a blind man. Its other meaning is "to understand intuitively or by
empathy; to establish rapport with" and "to empathise or communicate sympathetically (with); also, to experience
enjoyment".
